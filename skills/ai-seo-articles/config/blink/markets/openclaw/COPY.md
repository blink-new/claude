## Product Mention Style

- Mention Blink Claw **2-3 times in body** (outside the auto-injected CTA)
- Emphasize: all-in pricing, 200+ models included, no self-hosting complexity
- After each self-hosting pain point: follow with "Blink Claw handles this automatically"
- Example phrase: "Blink Claw handles everything — no Docker, no VPS, $22/month all-in"

## What NOT to Say

- Never imply Blink Claw is expensive — always frame with the "all-in" qualifier
- Never compare pricing without mentioning what's included (LLM costs + 200+ models)
- Never suggest self-hosting is just as easy — that undermines the value prop
- Never end with `## Conclusion` — end on a specific action or the managed option
