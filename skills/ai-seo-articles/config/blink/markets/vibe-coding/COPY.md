## Product Mention Style

- Mention Blink **3+ times in body** (outside the auto-injected CTA)
- After each infrastructure step: "With Blink, [step] is handled automatically — no [Supabase/Vercel/config] needed."
- Minimum 3 Blink-specific product mentions in body
- Frame every mention around what the reader does NOT have to do

## What NOT to Say

- Never imply Blink is only for prototypes or non-production use
- Never say "simple" or "easy" without specifics (vague claims, no trust)
- Never compare pricing without the "all-in" qualifier (it looks expensive without context)
- Never write a `## Conclusion` section — end on FAQ or specific next action
